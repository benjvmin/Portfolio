Tools Utilized?

Great question! I currently code using Adobe's Brackets in macOS, with many plugins to ease the process of repetitive work. These plugins are anything from an autoprefixer, to creating a barebones HTML skeleton. I use another piece of software called Prepros, that has useful functions such as live reload, Sass compiling, and minification. I use ImageOptim and Adobe Photoshop for correctly editing and optimizing images. My preferred design program is Adobe Illustrator, but I am always looking to see how I can utilize new types of software to improve my workflow.

PRINT.

An extensive background in print design has a profound impact on the way I approach digital mediums. Therefore, it remains an effective tool when approaching and solving problems. The use of grid systems, color theory, effective typography, and the process of accommodating document specifications has transformed my skillset moving forward into the web design realm.

WEB.

Ever since I was introduced to the underlying groundwork of a web page when I was young, I have always found myself impassioned by the creation and implementation of the web's endless possibilities. I felt that it always has been my calling to further challenge myself to create beautiful, useful, and semantic components on the web. I am always eager to learn how I can improve.

DESIGN.

Unlike many developers, I heavily stress the composition of elements within any work I touch. While a daunting task at first, becoming a hybrid designer & developer augments my ability to extend my imagination to places previously improbable. Design is built into my foundation as a front end developer and is at the forefront of every single problem I am engaged in solving.
